Cam:

Co-Founder and whatever




Hunter Says,

"

For starters, I saw a void in the music market place in Jackson, TN. My entreprenurial nature kicked in, and I decided to serve as a vinyl dealer inside my friend's bookstore downtown...

Once he moved locations, I started renting his old spot and became the only brick and mortar record shop between Memphis and Nashville...

Music on vinyl seems to transcend age and gender. We believe in being reverential to music on a physical format such as vinyl while also acting as purveyors of the album as an artform.

"
